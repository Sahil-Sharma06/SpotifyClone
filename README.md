# SpotifyClone

**LocalBeats - Your Offline Music Companion**

LocalBeats is a lightweight, user-friendly Spotify clone built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. What sets it apart is its unique focus on local music stored on your device. With LocalBeats, you can effortlessly manage and play your favorite tunes without the need for an internet connection.
